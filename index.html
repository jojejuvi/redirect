About the smiledirect.com GROUP

What is the smiledirect.com GROUP actually?

The smiledirect.com GROUP is a company that operates many successful online shops and sells high-quality products and services to its private and business customers.

Smiledirect is a direct manufacturer and produces the products sold via the online shops itself on
a production area of ​​over 16,500 square meters in 3 plants.

Which brands belong to the smiledirect.com GROUP?

Many strong online brands belong to the smiledirect.com GROUP.
This also includes postersmile®.de for individual photo products for private customers, as well as the associated B2B solution artsmile™.de for dealers, photographers and galleries.

The German market leader geosmile®.de for geography articles for office use and several Amazon.de bestsellers No. 1 are also represented in the group.

WENSCHOW Verlag has been supplying schools and universities with all kinds of maps and world maps since 1918 using the patented technique of drawn reliefs and has been part of the smiledirect.com GROUP for many years.

Parcel7 is responsible for sales and receivables management for many large online shops.

Many other exciting projects are already on the home stretch of realization and will soon also be part of the smiledirect.com GROUP.

Which products or services do brands of the smiledirect.com GROUP offer?

Private customers:
The smiledirect.com GROUP is a direct manufacturer of individual photo products of all kinds, specializes in photo canvases, among other things, and benefits from its own canvas coating system from 08/2022 and its own wooden stretcher frame production (19/38mm x 45 -60mm) as well as our own cardboard production (E / B / C / BC) with flexographic printing.
This enables a production capacity of 800,000 photo canvases per month .

B2B:
We also pass on this enormous capacity to wholesaler customers and other dealers, galleries and photographers in the form of a 100% white label solution with direct shipping to their end customers. These services are processed via artsmile.de .

Geography: Geography
articles such as globes and world maps are offered via the sales lines geosmile® and WENSCHOW for the purpose of school or university further education or for office use. All photo products and a large part of the geography articles are produced on a production area of ​​more than 16,500 square meters in 3 plants.

Where is the smiledirect.com GROUP located?

The smiledirect.com GROUP is based in the heart of Bavaria in Freilassing, directly adjacent to Salzburg, the metropolis of world art in our neighboring country Austria.

How many customers does the smiledirect.com GROUP have so far?

The history of the smiledirect.com GROUP goes way back to the year 2001. During this period, the smiledirect.com GROUP was able to build up a strong name and record

many successes with its online sales channels such as geosmile®.de and postersmile®.de and build up a customer base of over 2,500,000 customers throughout Germany, but also internationally.
In addition, more than 3,000 active wholesalers/dealers/artists/photographers are already benefiting from artsmile's white label solution for photos on canvas and other individually manufactured photo items.

smiledirect also for business customers in the B2B area?

Definitive!

More than 3,000
active wholesalers/dealers/artists/photographers are already regularly benefiting from the white label solution for photos on canvas and
other individually manufactured photo items via the sales line artsmile.de.


Artsmile enables business customers to order photo canvases with their own logo on the back of the canvas
and have them delivered directly to their own customers, thus pursuing the very aspiring
and increasingly popular drop-shipping principle.

Producing yourself is no longer worthwhile for most dealers, since the purchase price at artsmile is usually lower
than if the dealer produces his own.

You can easily specify your individual logo lettering for the back of your canvas, which
will be automatically applied to each subsequent order.
You also have the option of having your individual delivery note with your own logo
delivered to your customers, as well as having your own company/artist name
printed on the DHL/DPD package label.

All without additional fees or a monthly fee.
